Who We Are

Full Exposure Photography is a photography studio that simply hopes to share our love of the art with the world. We are three retired gentlement who have over 50 years of professional photography experience between us.

Growing up with far less resources than the people of this generation, we want to do a bit to share some of our work. It is our hope that people will see our work and be inspired to share their work and grow as photographers.

We wish to provide resources, tutorials, and some sample photography but we also have an online shop for anyone who wants to support us and hang up our work.
